About Me

Currently, I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics and Statistics at Forward College Lisbon, where I have been faculty since 2024. My research bridges representation theory, harmonic analysis, and algebraic structures, and I am currently exploring potential connections with Quantum Information Theory.

My academic journey includes a BOF Postdoctoral Fellowship at Ghent University (2021–2024), where I held a research-only appointment focusing on algebraic dualities and deformations. Prior to that, from 2016 to 2021, I was a Postdoctoral Research Assistant at the University of Oxford, where I conducted research in Dirac operators, harmonic analysis, and the Langlands Programme, alongside teaching tutorials at Somerville and Lincoln Colleges.

I completed my PhD in 2016 as a joint degree from the University of Amsterdam and Aix-Marseille University, under the supervision of E. Opdam and V. Heiermann.

You can explore my latest research interests, papers, and open-source implementations using the menus above.
